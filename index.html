Web Development

Web development is the skill of creating websites.

Why companies need websites.

  • A website is used for advertising a company to the rest of the world.
  • A website offers different information to people interested in the company without them having to reach tha company.
  • A website can have many functions like transactions which make it easier for investors to invest and access money.
  • A website can be used in cases where a company needs to give information that it can't deliver to people in person.
